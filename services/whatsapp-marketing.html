    Common Questions About WhatsApp Marketing

    How quickly can I start seeing results?

    WhatsApp marketing delivers immediate results. You can see engagement and conversions within 24-48 hours of campaign launch. We provide real-time analytics and optimization.

    What types of campaigns can you create?

    We create various campaigns including promotional messages, customer support automation, appointment booking, order confirmations, and personalized marketing campaigns.

    How do you measure WhatsApp marketing success?

    We track delivery rates, open rates, response rates, conversion rates, click-through rates, and overall ROI. Monthly reports show detailed performance metrics.

    What is the cost of WhatsApp marketing?

    Costs vary based on message volume, campaign complexity, and automation requirements. We offer customized packages starting from $500/month. Contact us for detailed pricing.

    Do you provide WhatsApp Business API setup?

    Yes, we handle the complete WhatsApp Business API setup, verification process, and integration with your existing systems and CRM platforms.

    Is WhatsApp marketing compliant with regulations?

    Absolutely! We ensure all campaigns comply with WhatsApp's policies, GDPR, and local regulations. We implement proper opt-in/opt-out mechanisms and data protection measures.
