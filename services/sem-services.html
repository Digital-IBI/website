    Frequently Asked Questions

    Common Questions About SEM Services

    How quickly can I see SEM results?

    SEM provides immediate results. You can start seeing traffic and conversions within 24-48 hours of campaign launch. We optimize continuously for better performance.

    What is the minimum budget for SEM?

    We recommend a minimum monthly budget of $1,000 for effective SEM campaigns. Budget allocation depends on your industry, competition, and goals.

    How do you measure SEM success?

    We track key metrics including click-through rates, conversion rates, cost per acquisition, return on ad spend (ROAS), and overall campaign ROI.

    Which platforms do you manage?

    We manage Google Ads, Microsoft Advertising, Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads, LinkedIn Ads, and other major advertising platforms based on your target audience.

    How often do you optimize campaigns?

    We monitor campaigns daily and perform optimizations weekly. Major strategy adjustments are made monthly based on performance data and market trends.

    Do you provide transparent reporting?

    Yes, we provide detailed monthly reports with campaign performance, spend analysis, conversion tracking, and recommendations for improvement.
