    Frequently Asked Questions

    Common Questions About App Development

    How long does app development take?

    Development time varies based on complexity. Simple apps take 6-12 weeks, while complex applications can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during planning.

    Do you develop for both iOS and Android?

    Yes, we develop for both platforms. We can create native apps for each platform or cross-platform solutions using React Native, Flutter, or Xamarin.

    What is the cost of app development?

    Costs vary based on features, complexity, and platforms. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements. Simple apps start from $15,000.

    Do you help with app store submission?

    Yes, we handle the entire app store submission process including store optimization, metadata preparation, and compliance requirements.

    What technologies do you use?

    We use Swift/Objective-C for iOS, Kotlin/Java for Android, React Native, Flutter for cross-platform, and modern backend technologies.

    How do you ensure app security?

    We implement industry-standard security practices including data encryption, secure APIs, regular security audits, and compliance with privacy regulations.
