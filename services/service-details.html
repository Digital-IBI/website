    Frequently Asked Questions

    Common Questions About Our Services

    How long does custom software development take?

    The timeline varies based on project complexity. Simple applications take 4-8 weeks, while complex enterprise solutions can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

    What technologies do you use?

    We use modern, scalable technologies including React, Angular, Node.js, Python, .NET, and cloud platforms like AWS and Azure. Technology selection depends on your specific requirements.

    Do you provide ongoing support?

    Yes, we offer comprehensive support packages including maintenance, updates, bug fixes, and feature enhancements. Support duration varies by package.

    Can you work with existing systems?

    Absolutely! We specialize in integrating new custom software with existing systems and databases. We ensure seamless compatibility and data migration.

    What is your development process?

    We follow an agile methodology with four main phases: Discovery & Planning, Design & Prototyping, Development & Testing, and Deployment & Support.

    How do you ensure quality and security?

    We implement rigorous testing protocols, code reviews, and security best practices. All applications undergo thorough QA testing and security audits before deployment.
