Distributor Order Frequency Increased by 21%

A global snack manufacturer struggled with manual distributor ordering processes, leading to errors, delays, and poor distributor engagement. Their traditional paper-based system was inefficient and limited growth opportunities.

The Challenge

The global snack manufacturer faced critical B2B challenges:

  • Manual paper-based ordering processes
  • High error rates in order processing
  • Poor distributor engagement and satisfaction
  • Limited visibility into distributor activities
  • Slow order processing and fulfillment
  • No real-time inventory updates

The Solution

Infetech developed a comprehensive distributor engagement portal:

  • Self-Service Portal: Automated ordering system for distributors
  • Real-Time Inventory: Live stock availability and updates
  • Order Management: Streamlined ordering and tracking
  • Analytics Dashboard: Performance insights and reporting
  • Mobile Optimization: Responsive design for field teams
  • Integration: Seamless ERP and CRM connectivity
