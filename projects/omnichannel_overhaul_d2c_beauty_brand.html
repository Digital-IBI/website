57% Lift in Online Revenue with Omnichannel Strategy

A direct-to-consumer beauty brand struggled with fragmented customer experiences across multiple channels, leading to low conversion rates and poor customer retention. Their siloed approach to ecommerce and social media was limiting growth potential.

The Challenge

The D2C beauty brand faced critical omnichannel challenges:

  • Disconnected customer experiences across channels
  • Low conversion rates on social media
  • Poor customer service response times
  • Limited integration between Shopify and Instagram
  • No unified customer data platform
  • Inconsistent brand experience

The Solution

Infetech implemented a comprehensive omnichannel overhaul:

  • Shopify Storefront: Optimized ecommerce platform with enhanced UX
  • Instagram Shopping: Integrated product catalog and checkout
  • AI Chat Support: Implemented intelligent customer service
  • Unified Customer Data: Centralized customer profiles and preferences
  • Social Commerce: Seamless shopping from social media
  • Personalization Engine: AI-driven product recommendations
