Smart-Meter Data Platform Processes 10M Readings Daily

A major power utility struggled with inefficient meter data collection, manual reading processes, and limited insights into energy consumption patterns. Their legacy systems couldn't handle the massive volume of smart-meter data needed for optimization.

The Challenge

The power utility faced critical data management challenges:

  • Manual meter reading processes causing delays and errors
  • Inability to process 10M daily meter readings efficiently
  • Limited predictive maintenance capabilities
  • No real-time energy consumption analytics
  • Poor integration between smart meters and billing systems
  • High operational costs due to inefficient processes

The Solution

Infetech developed a comprehensive smart-meter data platform:

  • IoT Data Processing: Real-time processing of 10M daily readings
  • Predictive Analytics: AI-powered maintenance and consumption insights
  • Mobile App Development: Field technician applications for meter management
  • Real-Time Dashboard: Live energy consumption monitoring
  • Database Optimization: High-performance data storage and retrieval
  • Energy Management: Automated optimization algorithms
