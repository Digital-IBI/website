IoT Smart-Factory Dashboard Reduces Unplanned Downtime by 18%

A major electronics manufacturer struggled with unplanned equipment failures and inefficient maintenance scheduling. Their traditional reactive maintenance approach led to costly production delays, quality issues, and increased operational costs that impacted profitability and customer satisfaction.

The Challenge

The electronics OEM faced critical manufacturing challenges:

  • Unpredictable equipment failures causing production delays
  • Reactive maintenance approach leading to high costs
  • Limited visibility into equipment health and performance
  • Manual data collection from 1,200+ sensors across multiple facilities
  • No predictive maintenance capabilities
  • Quality issues due to equipment malfunctions

The Solution

Infetech developed a comprehensive IoT Smart-Factory Dashboard solution:

  • Real-time Data Integration: Connected 1,200+ sensors across manufacturing facilities
  • Predictive Analytics Engine: Implemented ML algorithms for equipment failure prediction
  • Interactive Dashboard: Created real-time monitoring interface with alerts
  • Maintenance Optimization: Automated maintenance scheduling based on predictive insights
  • Quality Control Integration: Linked equipment health to product quality metrics
  • Mobile Alerts: Instant notifications for critical equipment issues

Key Features Implemented

  • Real-time sensor data collection and processing
  • Predictive maintenance algorithms with 95% accuracy
  • Interactive dashboard with drill-down capabilities
  • Automated alert system for equipment health
  • Maintenance scheduling optimization
  • Quality correlation analysis
  • Mobile-responsive monitoring interface
