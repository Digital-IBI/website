A global CPG conglomerate struggled with slow website performance across multiple regions, leading to poor user experience, high bounce rates, and lost sales opportunities. Their existing infrastructure couldn't handle the global traffic demands effectively.

The Challenge

The CPG conglomerate faced critical performance challenges:

  • 6-second page load times across global markets
  • High bounce rates due to slow performance
  • Poor user experience in emerging markets
  • Limited scalability during peak traffic periods
  • No CDN optimization for global delivery
  • Lost revenue due to performance issues

The Solution

Infetech implemented a comprehensive multi-region e-commerce infrastructure:

  • CloudFront CDN: Deployed global content delivery network
  • Lambda@Edge: Implemented edge computing for dynamic content
  • Multi-Region Architecture: Distributed infrastructure across continents
  • Performance Optimization: Image compression and lazy loading
  • Global Load Balancing: Intelligent traffic distribution
  • Real-time Monitoring: Performance tracking across regions
