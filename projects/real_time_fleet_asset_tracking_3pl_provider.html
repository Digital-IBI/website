Real-Time Fleet & Asset Tracking Reduces Idle-Time by 19%

A major third-party logistics (3PL) provider struggled with inefficient fleet management, lost shipments, and poor asset visibility across their supply chain network. Their traditional tracking methods couldn't provide real-time insights needed for optimization.

The Challenge

The 3PL provider faced critical logistics challenges:

  • High fleet idle-time reducing operational efficiency
  • Lost shipment incidents affecting customer satisfaction
  • Limited real-time visibility into asset locations
  • Manual tracking processes causing delays
  • No predictive analytics for route optimization
  • Poor integration between GPS and RFID systems

The Solution

Infetech developed a comprehensive real-time tracking solution:

  • GPS Integration: Real-time fleet tracking with advanced GPS technology
  • RFID Asset Tracking: Automated asset identification and monitoring
  • AI Analytics Platform: Predictive insights for route optimization
  • Mobile App Development: Cross-platform tracking application
  • Real-Time Dashboard: Live monitoring and alert system
  • Supply Chain Integration: Seamless connectivity with existing systems
