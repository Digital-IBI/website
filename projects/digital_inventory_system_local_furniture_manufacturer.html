Digital Inventory System Cuts Stock-Outs by 33%

A local furniture manufacturer struggled with manual inventory management, frequent stock-outs, and poor order accuracy. Their traditional paper-based system couldn't keep up with growing production demands and customer expectations.

The Challenge

The furniture manufacturer faced critical inventory challenges:

  • Frequent stock-outs affecting production schedules
  • Manual inventory tracking causing errors and delays
  • Poor order accuracy leading to customer dissatisfaction
  • No real-time visibility into stock levels
  • Inefficient barcode scanning processes
  • Limited integration with production systems

The Solution

Infetech implemented a comprehensive digital inventory system:

  • Barcode-Based Management: Automated inventory tracking with barcode scanning
  • Real-Time Monitoring: Live stock level updates and alerts
  • Mobile App Development: Cross-platform inventory management application
  • Predictive Analytics: AI-powered demand forecasting
  • Order Management: Automated order processing and fulfillment
  • Production Integration: Seamless connectivity with manufacturing systems
