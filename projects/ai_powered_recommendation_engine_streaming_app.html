AI-Powered Recommendation Engine for Streaming App

A leading streaming platform struggled with content discovery and user engagement. Their existing recommendation system was basic and couldn't provide personalized content suggestions, leading to low watch-time and high churn rates.

The Challenge

The platform had millions of users but poor content discovery mechanisms:

  • Low user engagement and watch-time
  • High churn rate due to poor content discovery
  • Inefficient content recommendation algorithms
  • Limited personalization capabilities
Our Solution

Advanced ML Models with Real-Time Inference

Intelligent content discovery powered by machine learning.

We developed a sophisticated recommendation engine using collaborative filtering, content-based filtering, and deep learning models. The system processes user behavior in real-time to provide highly personalized content suggestions.

Real-time personalization with 95% recommendation accuracy.
