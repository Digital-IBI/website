Unified Commerce Platform for Fashion Retailer

A leading multi-brand fashion house faced challenges with their fragmented ecommerce infrastructure across multiple brands. Their existing WooCommerce setup couldn't handle the scale and complexity of their operations, leading to poor performance during peak seasons and inconsistent customer experiences.

The Challenge

The client operated 12 different fashion brands, each with its own website, inventory system, and customer database. This fragmentation led to:

  • Inconsistent customer experiences across brands
  • Poor performance during seasonal peaks (99% uptime issues)
  • Complex inventory management across multiple systems
  • Limited scalability for growth
Our Solution

Headless Shopify + Custom Middleware Architecture

Unified platform with seamless brand experiences.

We designed and implemented a headless commerce architecture using Shopify Plus as the backend, with a custom React-based frontend and middleware layer for unified operations. This solution provided the scalability and performance needed for their multi-brand operations.

Single platform managing all 12 brands with unified customer data and inventory.
